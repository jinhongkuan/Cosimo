The Problem

Semantic Gap

Goals get lost in translation. What you want to achieve (objectives) and how you'll get there (deliverables) live in different mental spaces. Traditional task managers track actions but lose the why.

What it does

Cosimo captures the semantic bridge between objectives and deliverables—the reasoning that explains why an action achieves a goal.

How it works

When deliverables link to multiple objectives, Cosimo surfaces high-leverage actions. One task that advances three goals gets prioritized over three tasks that each advance one.

Security

End-to-End Encryption

What it does

Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device. The server only stores encrypted blobs—even we can't read your goals.

How it works

Claude Code/Desktop spawns a local MCP server that handles AES-256 encryption. Your passphrase stays on your machine as an environment variable.
